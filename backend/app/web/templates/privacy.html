Privacy Policy

Last updated: this is a template — review with counsel before public launch.

What we access

With your permission, Filtration uses Google's read-only Gmail scope (gmail.readonly) to read messages so it can check them for fraud. We never send, delete, or modify your email.

What we store

We do not store the contents of your emails. For each scanned message we keep only: the sender name and address, the subject, the date, the computed risk score, and the reasons for that score. Your Google authorization token is stored encrypted and is used only to read your inbox when you scan.

Limited Use

Filtration's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Email data is used solely to provide the fraud-detection feature and is never sold, used for advertising, or used to train generalized AI models.

Third parties

Ambiguous messages may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis, subject to Anthropic's data-handling terms. Only the sanitized message content needed for classification is sent.

Deleting your data

Sign out to end the session, or contact us to delete your account and all stored results.
